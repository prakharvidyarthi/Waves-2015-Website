  1. Indian Rock

    If rock music is your forte, this where you need to be. The perfect platform to provide you with an opportunity to showcase your talent in rock music, albeit the Indian way. Let any Indian language bring to us the magic of your music.Performances can be known songs or own compositions. Think you have what it takes to be the true rockstar? Come show us.

    Rules

    Indian Rock Rules

    Category:Gold

    Team composition: 4 – 10 members

    Teams per college: Unlimited

    Registration: Online/On the spot

    Time limit:

           Eliminations: 10 minutes

           Finals: 15 minutes

      General Rules:


    • The songs should have lyrics in Indian languages. However, a rap with English lyrics is allowed.

    • Original compositions are allowed. In this case, 3 copies of the lyrics must be submitted at the time of registration on campus

    • In case of lyrics in any Indian languag other than Hindi , 3 copies of the literal translation of the lyrics in English must be e-mailed at events@bits-waves.org

    • Lyrics will be checked for vulgarity and profanity

    • Every extra minute taken beyond specified time limit will lead to reduction of 5% of the total marks scored

    • Usage of at least one percussion instrument is compulsory

    • A minimum of one vocalist is compulsory

    • There should be a minimum of four performing members on the stage at any particular point in time

    • Any pre-recorded music will lead to disqualification

    • No marks from the elimination roundwill be carried over to the finals

    • Availability of the following instruments (if required) is assured:Drum set, an electric Bass, a Keyboard, a Congo and a Tabla set.Any other instrument has to be bought by the participants

    • Judging criteria will include:

      1. Rhythm
      2. Sur
      3. Pitching
      4. Choice of songs
      5. Overall performance
      6. Background, Score

      Elimination Round:


    • The time limit for this round is 10 minutes, including the setup time

    • A minimum of one song has to be performed within the given time

    • The time limit will be strictly adhered to even if the team plays more than one song

    • Only one song from the elimination round may be performed in the finals

      Final Round:


    • A total of 7 teams will qualify for the finals

    • The time limit for this round is 15 minutes, including setup time

    • A minimum of two songs have to be performed

    • One song of the two has to be an original arrangement/improvisation of a Bollywood song.

    • The improvisation should include a short instrumental piece

    • Bonus points will be awarded if the instrumental piece is an original composition

    • This song can also be a mashup of maximum two songs

    • The vocalists may choose to hum,whistle, sing Taranaas, Sargam or in Aakar but cannot sing discrete words during the instrumental piece

    • The other song can be the one performed in eliminations. However, it could be a new song as well

  1. Sizzle

    If your feet can set the stage on fire, if your moves can astound crowds, if you think you are the complete performer, then Waves beckons you. This grueling multi round dance competition has embarked on the search for the foremost dancer there is.Let your dance speak for itself as you “Sizzle” because all you need is nothing short of extraordinary.

    Rules

    Sizzle Rules

    Category:Gold

    Team composition: 1 member

    Teams per college: Unlimited

    Registration: Online/On the spot

    Time limit:

           Eliminations: 2-3 minutes

           Finals: 3-5 minutes

      General Rules:


    • No classical dance forms are allowed

    • The soundtracks of the prepared sequences must be submitted to the event manager in a pen drive, prior to the event

      Elimination Round:


    • Participants will be required to perform a 2 minute sequence out of their prepared 3-5 minutes set

    • The elimination round is not open to watch, and will be held in a closed room. Only the judging panel will be viewing the performances

    • Use of props is not allowed

      Final Round 1:


    • Participants will be required to perform their entire prepared set of 3-5 minutes

    • A negative marking of 5% of the total score will be awarded for each minute of exceeding the time limit

    • 12 participants will move from Round 1 to Round 2 of the finals

      Final Round 2:


    • This is a theme/prop round

    • Participants will be divided into groups of 3

    • One group will perform at a time,where all 3 participants in that group dance simultaneously on a tune provided to them

    • Each participant in the group has to perform within a given area on the stage. Each area corresponds to a particular theme/prop, and the participant will have to stick to the theme/prop assigned to him/her

    • At the signal of the judges, positions will be shuffled and the participants will have to continue in a new area with a new theme/prop

    • Each group will be shuffled thrice so that all participants in the same group get to use all three themes/props

    • If a participant steps out of the area, he/she shall be warned once. If the offence is repeated, the participant will be eliminated immediately

    • Top 2 performers in each group will move to the next round

      Final Round 3:


    • This is a round of footloose

    • Two participants will be called on stage simultaneously. The first participant dances to a song played on the spot while the other will stay still

    • At the signal of the judges, the first participant will stop and the second participant will immediately begin dancing to the continuation of the same song

    • The process will be repeated every time the song is changed. The starting sequence for each song will be performed by participants 1 and 2 alternately

    • In this way, the song will be changed several times while the participants dance in an alternating manner

  5. Wallstreet Fête

    Here’s to all you can negotiate and all you can buy. Fancy yourself as Wallstreet worthy? Show it to us this Waves. Get the gold folks!

    Rules

    Wallstreet Fête Rules

    Category:Gold

    Team composition: 4-6 members

    Teams per college: Unlimited

    Registration: Online/On the spot

      General Rules:


    • One fixed person in each team must stay at the table throughout the event. All the other members are free to move around and negotiate in the game

    • A total of 8 to 10 teams would be allowed to participate. This shall be at the discretion of the organisers

    • The team with the maximum profit wins the game


      Level 1 – Bullion Beatdown:


    • There will be 10 rounds, each comprising of 2 minutes

    • Half the teams will be given 2000 units of gold. The other half will be given -2000 units of gold. Base price of each unit is 200

    • The teams are free to trade amongst themselves. Each trade should involve buying/selling in multiples of 100 units. Any team can buy or sell any number of units in each round

    • The negotiators are expected to negotiate the selling/buying price and bring it closest to their requirements

    • The person at the table will officially negotiate with members of different teams and “handle” the transactions

    • In the end, every team is expected to bring the number of units down to zero, after all 10 rounds have been concluded and the team with the greatest profit wins


      Level 2 – Loan. Paper. Scissors:


    • The top few teams from round 1 would go to round 2

    • The prime objective of the round is to sell your products and earn as much profit as you can

    • The teams will be divided into poor,emerging and rich countries based on a draw of lots, wherein they will be given materials like papers, scissors, cutters, pens etc. and some money initially in different proportions to the three category of countries to form their products

    • The products will be a circle, triangle,rectangle and two complicated shapes which will be shown on the spot


    • The teams have to make the shapes and sell them to the banker present after every round

    • The price of each shape is fixed and will be told on the spot

    • Since the different materials required for production are divided separately among the countries, the different teams have to negotiate with each other for the instruments to make their products

    • There will be 9 rounds totally. After each round teams have to sell their products to the banker present and at the same time their other team members can negotiate with other teams

    • Judging criteria:

      1. Taste
      2. Justice to the ingredients used
      3. The core ingredient, if any,should shine
      4. Plating and appearance

    • Depending on the number of entries, eliminations will be conducted on the basis of this


      The Team Challenge:


    • The participants will be split into 3 teams, by a draw of lots

    • A voting session will follow and the team standing last shall be eliminated

    • This shall be followed by an impromptu elimination round


      The Finale:


    • The finale shall be conducted in two rounds:

      1. Mystery Box Challenge

      2. Invention Test

    • Mystery Box Challenge:

      1. The finalists are given a box containing an odd combination of ingredients

      2. They are required to prepare a dish using some/all of these ingredients in an hour

      3. The person with the maximum score gets to choose the core ingredient for the next round

    • Invention Test:

      1. The winner of the mystery box challenge gets to pick the core ingredient for this round,from a choice of 3 ingredients

      2. The participants are now required to prepare a dish,using this core ingredient

      3. The duration of this round would be an hour

      4. The cumulative scores of the mystery box challenge and the invention test decide the winner
